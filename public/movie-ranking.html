The Best Movies According to Andy

I've watched these over and over

It's not easy picking a top three when it comes to movies, I love so many. I can say that I do have favourite genres. These include animation, thriller, end-of-the-world type stuff (bonus points if there's a psychological thriller vibe to it like Don't Look Up or that one with Julia Roberts and the ship). I also love RomComs and action movies a whole lot. Hmm... maybe I don't have favourites at all.

1. World War Z

Honestly, any end-of-the-world, humans-are-messing-up kind of movies are very close to the top of my list. I watched this one so much that I noticed every cut scene when they changed the version that was available, and I was unnecessarily upset about it. Along with this one are movies like I mentioned above and 2012 and The Day After Tomorrow. Bonus points if the dialogue is witty. I'm a huge fan of of catastrophic climate change movies.

2. Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse

"Don't call me [kid]"
"There it is"
"Hobie you're not helping"
"Good"
This is possibly my favourite part of the entire movie and I will be taking no questions because how do I even explain that? I was going to choose a RomCom for number two and trust me, RomComs are up there. However, this movie blew my socks off. I love the combination of animation styles, the rhetoric around politics and what is considered good, and who decides when "good" takes it a little too far. Such a good movie. AND THE END?! Big fan.

How to Train Your Dragon

These movies make me cry EVERY time I watch them. Honestly, if we were talking top genres, animation would undoubtedly be number one on my list. It's hard to choose a favourite animation, but this one really holds a special place in my heart. Maybe it's because Hiccup has the same identity crisis in every one of them, just in different fonts. I love that reflection of how not linear healing is.
